The EcoLoop planter

Built for the cramped reality of urban apartments and dorms, EcoLoop replaces bulky standalone hardware with a single planter that cleans air, manages moisture, and fits naturally into your space.

Why it exists. In shoebox apartments, opening a window is rarely enough to deal with dust, pollutants, and allergens. Traditional purifiers take up valuable room and can lose up to 50% of effective airflow when pushed into corners. EcoLoop solves that by integrating air care into an object you actually want visible.

How it works. A built-in dehumidifier pulls moisture from the air and stores it to automatically irrigate the plant. HEPA filtration captures airborne particles, and a selective membrane chamber converts collected waste into hydrogen gas to help power the system, with sunlight supporting the membrane reaction.

  • One object replaces separate purifier, dehumidifier, and extra floor clutter
  • Designed for active placement, not corner dead zones with blocked airflow
  • Built-in watering loop: air moisture -> storage -> plant soil
  • HEPA capture plus membrane conversion of collected waste into hydrogen gas
  • Cleaner living simplified for busy student and early-career routines
