Clean air that belongs in the room.

EcoLoop integrates air filtration and dehumidification into a design-forward plant pot—so improving indoor air quality does not mean living with a bulky box in the corner.

Form & function

Built for renters and shared spaces: air care that looks intentional on a shelf or table, not like afterthought hardware.

No dead zones

Research shows corner-placed purifiers can lose up to half their effective coverage when airflow is blocked. EcoLoop is designed to live where you actually use it.

↻

Automated care

Dehumidified water is collected, stored, and released to the plant on demand—less guesswork, steadier conditions for both air and soil.

Mission. EcoLoop seeks to reinvent the indoor space. Ensuring a clean and safe living environment shouldn’t have to be actively achieved, but rather automated and guaranteed.
