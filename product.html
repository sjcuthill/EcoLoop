The EcoLoop planter

A design-forward plant pot that combines air purification and dehumidification—built for shared apartments and dorms where traditional purifiers are too loud, too ugly, or stuck in corners that kill coverage.

What it does. EcoLoop improves indoor air quality while supporting the plant on top. A compact, fist-sized two-chamber system inside the pot handles filtration logic: particle waste is converted into hydrogen gas so the unit can run without relying on an external feed you have to manage every week.

Plant care, automated. Moisture pulled from the air by the dehumidifier is collected, stored, and released to the soil when the plant needs it—reducing maintenance and avoiding the feast-or-famine cycle of manual watering.

  • Integrated purification and dehumidification in one piece of furniture-adjacent design
  • Addresses “dead zone” risk: corner purifiers can lose ~50% effective coverage when airflow is blocked
  • Lower visual and spatial footprint than typical standalone purifiers
  • Hydrogen generation from processed particle waste for more independent operation
  • Water recovery loop from dehumidifier → storage → plant
